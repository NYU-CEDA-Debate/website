2022-23 ELITE EIGHT

The 2022-23 IPPF involved qualifying round submissions from more than 220 teams, representing schools in 22 countries and 31 U.S. states. Those essays were judged, with the help of the NYU CEDA team, and the top 64 teams were invited to engage in a single-elimination, written debate tournament. By April, the field was narrowed to the final eight teams, who were invited to compete in oral debates in New York City. Here are the results of those debates.

QUARTERS

  • Potomac Oak advanced over Mountain View-Los Altos Speech and Debate
  • Mountain Vista advanced over Wasatch Independent Debate League
  • EEC Debate advanced over Notre Dame San Jose
  • The Hockaday School advanced over UWC South East Asia

SEMIFINAL RESULTS

  • The Hockaday School advanced over Mountain Vista High School
  • Potomac Oak advanced over EEC Debate

FINALS

  • Potomac Oak defeated
